Tempeh Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Tempeh
  • 1 Whole Bell Pepper
  • 1 Whole Onion
  • 1 Can of Beans of your choice
  • 1 Can of Corn
  • Taco Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Chop the bell pepper and onion to whatever size you'd like. I generally dice them
  2. Heat up some oil on your frying pan and cook the bell pepper and onions to your desired level
  3. Crumble the tempeh into small chunks.
  4. Mix in the tempeh with the bell pepper and onions.
  5. Mix in the desired amount of beans and corn
  6. Add taco seasoning to taste. I generally like a lot!

Pasta Sauce

Ingredients

  • Jar of your favorite pasta or marinara sauce
  • Minced Garlic
  • 1 Whole Onions
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Whole Bell Pepper
  • 1 Can of Corn
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Meat substitute (tempeh, fake ground beef, and/or tofurky)

Instructions

  1. Pour the sauce into a pot or slow cooker. I personally like using the slow cooker.
  2. Put the garlic, onions, and bell peppers, and italian seasoning to your liking
  3. Add your meat subtitute of choice.
  4. Simmer for 1-2 hours in a regular pot on low-medium heat or 6 hours on low in the slow cooker
  5. Slice the cherry tomatoes in half and add the tomatoes and corn w/ 30-60 minutes left in cooking time

Learning Material

  • The China Study by Thomas M. Campbell
